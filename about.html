A Bit About The Blog

A travel and food blog created between two travel and food lovers! We hope to share some of the best foods we've tasted on our travels and inspire you to give these places a go too! We pick a few of our favourite foods to try on each trip and where you can find it.

A Bit About Us

Nicole Kakay
I was born and bred in Southern England with Vietnamese and French parents. Growing up around a mixture of cuisines and methods to cooking has fuelled my love for tasty food! I work in Aerospace Defence and I'm fortunate enough to travel a lot for my job so I try to taste as many different things whilst I'm away. When I'm not eating or playing Top Gun, I like to play tennis or learn to code! A complete Londoner, I grew up in the very center of this big city: Covent Garden. I am very passionate about travelling the world and exploring different cultures and their cusines, 9 countries in 2017, let's hope 2018 is even better! My day job is just as interesting - I work as a Business Analyst in Robotics for Risk in the City but my other interests lie in: coding, hiking, cooking and fitness!
